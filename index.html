What is Gematria?

Gematria is a numerology system that assigns numerical values to words or phrases, revealing connections between them. Rooted in Jewish tradition, it’s widely used in Kabbalah and Rabbinic texts. Learn more on our About Gematria page or explore the systems on our Ciphers page.

How to Use This Tool
